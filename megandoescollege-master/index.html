about me...

Megan is a graduate from UC Berkeley (2018) where she earned a bachelor’s degree in Political Science, and has been working as a College Counselor and Essay Editor ever since. In 2021, she completed her Certificate in College Admissions and Career Planning through UC Berkeley Extension.
